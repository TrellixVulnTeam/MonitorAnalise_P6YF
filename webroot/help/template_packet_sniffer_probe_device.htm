PRTG Manual: Template Packet Sniffer Probe Device

<%NUMBERING1%>.<%NUMBERING2%>.<%NUMBERING3%> PRTG Manual: Template Packet Sniffer Probe Device

By default, you can only monitor traffic passing the PRTG probe system where the Probe Device with the sensor is set up (either a local or remote probe). To monitor other traffic in your network, you can configure a monitoring port (if available) that the switch sends a copy of all traffic to. You can then physically connect this port to a network card of the computer the PRTG probe (either local or remote probe) is running on. This way, PRTG can analyze the complete traffic that passes through the switch. This feature of your hardware may be called Switched Port Analyzer (SPAN), port mirroring, or port monitoring.
