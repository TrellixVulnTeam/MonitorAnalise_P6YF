<%NUMBERING1%>.<%NUMBERING2%>.<%NUMBERING3%> PRTG Manual: Template Lookup Enable Alerting

To receive alerts based on the lookup definition, you have to enable alerting in the channel settings, section Alerting and Limits, after you have added the sensor. By default, alerting is disabled so error and warning states defined in the lookup will not apply if you do not enable alerting.
